ABOUT G.G.P, ALMORA
Government Girl’s Polytechnic, Almora, established in 1987,
recently celebrated it’s 35th anniversary of imparting high quality
education to the students of Uttrakhand.

G.G.P.Almora is approved by AICTE & associated
with Uttarakhand board of technical education, Roorkee.

Every year an entrance exam is conducted by U.B.T.E.
